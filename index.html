OUR CITY:SAMYAK CITY

Samyak city is a smart well-structured city planned near Madikeri outskirts, blending modern living with kodagu natural beauty. It features clean industries, smart infrastructures, rich cultural spaces and green living with good transportation, eco-tourism spots and the city aims to create better job opportunities and employment while preserving green and open landscapes. Also it aims to provide good healthcare and education.
