Business Case Studies

1. Impact of Solar Energy Adoption on Property Value

This case study evaluates how rooftop solar panel installations affect residential property prices, buyer demand, return on investment, and sustainability branding in modern housing developments.

2. Smart Suburban Community Development

Focuses on planning technology-driven neighborhoods using IoT systems such as smart meters, EV charging stations, and digital security infrastructure.

3. Digital Transformation in Banking

Examines how mobile banking, UPI systems, automation, and AI reduce operational costs while improving customer experience in the banking sector.

4. Cybersecurity & Fraud Prevention in FinTech

Analyzes the rise of digital fraud and the role of AI-based fraud detection, biometric authentication, and secure transaction frameworks.

5. Cultural Influence on Consumer Behavior

Explores how cultural and religious values shape consumer choices in food, fashion, tourism, and festival-based markets.

6. Sustainable Practices in the Hospitality Industry

Studies eco-friendly hotel operations such as energy efficiency, waste management, green certifications, and their effect on brand reputation.
